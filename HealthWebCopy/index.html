          Why us?

The best part about this app is what makes it unique, which is the fact that most companies include a paid subscription to access extra tools within their apps. Ours is completely free, in the sense that it is covered by sponsors and advertisements, so there is no need for someone who could be making minimum wage, to have to spend money every month on an app when it can be used on more important things like rent or food, or his kids.

What can you do with this?

Our app is dedicated to promoting fitness and sending out reminders to ensure that meals are being eaten properly. This app will contain reminders to make sure that your water and food intake is being done properly, and even recommends him some meals that are creative and just as effectiveto make.

Future Expansion

This is intended to become an app one day, taking advantage of the mobility and ease of access to those with phones and watches to assist them in achieving their dietary goals.
